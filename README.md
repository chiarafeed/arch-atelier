🦊 Arch-Atelier(WIP)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-work--in--progress-orange)

    The Arch Linux installer that doesn't hate you, won't wipe Windows, and gives you Pokémon.

Tired of cold installers that leave you in a black desert (looking at you, Hyprland) or make your Windows bootloader vanish like it's nothing? Welcome home.
🌟 Why this project?

Arch Linux is powerful, but its community can be a bit... exhausting. This installer is born for those who want the power of Arch without having to endure the "initiation rite of pain."
What's different:

    🛡️ Anti-Disaster Shield: It detects Windows, protects its bootloader, and backs up your EFI partition. If something goes wrong, you won't be left stranded.

    🎭 The Dressing Room (Live Preview): Want to try Hyprland? GNOME? KDE? I'll show them to you before you install them. Hop in, see how it looks, and if you don't like it, just hit a key and go back.

    🛠️ The Toolbox: If you choose a minimal environment (like Hyprland), I won't abandon you. I'll ask if you want Kitty, Waybar, or Dolphin—YOU decide what to check.

    🌈 Eye Candy Included: Want Pokémon in your terminal? Arch ASCII art at startup? It’s just one click away.

🚀 How to get the ISO

You don't need to compile anything. Go to the Releases section on the right, download the latest .iso, and flash it to a USB drive.
Ottimo! Se hai già fatto il push, ora il tuo repository è ufficialmente aggiornato. Avere i requisiti tecnici anche in inglese renderà il progetto ancora più credibile a livello internazionale.

🛠️ Minimum System Requirements (Live System)

To test the Desktop Environments in the Atelier without installing them:

    RAM: At least 4GB (8GB recommended for a smooth experience).

    Connection: Required to download packages during the live preview.

    USB: A flash drive with at least 4GB of space.

🧠 Why these specific numbers?

    RAM is your "Disk": When you test a system in RAM-only mode, everything you download or run doesn't go to the hard drive; it "floats" in volatile memory. 4GB is the bare minimum to prevent the system from crashing when you open a window.
